Why QA?

Over the past two years, I’ve worked as a content writer and administrator for a small affiliate marketing business. Beyond creating content, I was also responsible for reviewing our websites before they went live — checking for errors, ensuring quality, and working closely with our programmer. Combined with my natural attention to detail and love for structured problem-solving, QA felt like the perfect next step. Now, I’m pursuing this field with the goal of building a flexible, remote-friendly career where I can grow professionally while staying curious and detail-oriented.
