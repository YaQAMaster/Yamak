About

Surrounded by water and marine life, I developed a deep love for the ocean through swimming, diving, and water sports.

My dad owned a watersports business, teaching people how to water ski behind his MasterCraft boat.

That same environment sparked a lifelong passion for surfing — a sport that, at its core, is about patience, timing, and a sharp eye for detail.

Learning to surf taught me to notice small changes in conditions — shifts in wind, movement in the water, or subtle imperfections in technique — things that may seem invisible to the untrained eye, but make all the difference in performance. That mindset naturally led me to QA.

Like surfing, Quality Assurance is all about focus, precision, and spotting what others might miss. Today, I bring that same attention to detail to the digital world — catching bugs, refining systems, and making sure everything flows just right.

My dream is to work remotely and experience true freedom — combining my passion for QA with a lifestyle that lets me explore some of the world’s most beautiful surf spots.

To build a career where I can open my laptop a few meters from the waves, contribute meaningfully to a tech team, and ride out each day doing what I love — both on the screen and in the sea.
